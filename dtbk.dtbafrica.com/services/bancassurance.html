Career life is an education policy which provides a policyholder with saving plans such as planned saving for high school, college and university fees etc.

Benefits
  • Policy loan
  • Tax relief (15% of B.P.)
  • 30-day free-look period
  • Minimum of 4.5 interest per annum
Features
  • Entry age (ANB): 18-65 years
  • Maximum maturity age: 70 years
  • Minimum sum assured: Kshs. 200,000
  • Policy term can vary from 5 to 20 years
  • Cash value: 3 full year’s premiums
  • Must be in force for at least 3 years

Fanaka is an endowment policy which provides the policyholder with a disciplined savings plan ranging from planned savings for business capital, home upgrading, purchase of land among others.

Benefits
  • Policy loan
  • Tax relief (15% of B.P.)
  • 30-day free-look period
  • Minimum of 4.5 interest per annum
Features
  • Entry age (ANB): 18-65 years
  • Maximum maturity age: 70 years
  • Minimum sum assured: Kshs. 350,000
  • Cash value: 5-10 year terms, after 5 years and 11-20 year terms, after 3 years

The last expense cover gives your loved ones the much-needed financial support to attend to funeral preparations within 48 hours of the loss.

Benefits
  • No waiting period on accidental death
  • Claims paid within 24 hours
  • Three months waiting period for illness
  • Pays for any one death per year
  • Premium per family covered 5
  • Parent & parents in law covered at extra cost
  • Simple application process
Features
  • Single claim option
    • Annual cover Kshs. 50,000- 500,000
    • Annual premium (Nuclear Family) Kshs. 600- 5,976
    • Annual premium (per extra child) Kshs. 100- 900
    • Annual premium per parent Kshs. 612- 6,120
  • Multiple claim option (Up to three claims per year)
    • Annual cover Kshs. 50,000- 500,000
    • Annual premium (Nuclear Family) Kshs. 664- 6,576
    • Annual premium (per extra child) Kshs. 100- 900
    • Annual premium per parent Kshs. 612- 6,120
  • N.B: If you have Cancer, Diabetes, Hypertension, Stroke, Major Organ Failure/Organ Transplant, Heart Disease/Angina, HIV related Illnesses
    • Cover will be capped to a maximum of Kshs. 250,000 for the first year
    • For cover of Kshs. 500,000 only Kshs. 250,000 will be processed within 48 hrs. balance upon submission of original death certificate for the first year.
Registration Details and Requirements

We welcome you to register on the website

  • Copy of ID and pin of applicant
  • Copy of ID of spouse
  • Copy of ID of parent/ In-laws
  • Pay via Mpesa
  • Get cover note instantly to your email
  • Get instant SMS to your mobile phone
Exclusions
  • Active participation in war
  • Overdose by un-prescribed drugs
  • Extreme sports (Ziplining, mountain climbing, speed racing etc.)
  • Intentional self-injury
  • Suicide
  • Radio activity
